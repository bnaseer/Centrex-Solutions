Upstream Solutions

Upstream Energy companies are looking for ways to optimize their business and create more with less. Centrex Solutions working with their solution partners can assist their clients with building end to end Upstream solutions.

Cloud Services

Cloud solutions assist companies minimize their operational spend by moving their infrastructure offsite. However, moving to the cloud can be challenging if not done properly. Centrex Solutions can assist your organization identify common migration issues and manage the process to move your systems from on premise to the cloud.

Robotics Process Automation

Robotic Process Automation (RPA) is a programmable software system that automates repetitive clerical tasks—thereby freeing up your human resources for more strategic tasks. Centrex Solutions can assist your organization identify and build the best RPA solutions for your organization to maximize efficiency.

Machine Learning

Business Intelligence (BI) is taking big data and finding a way to find and discover important information to assist your organization make important decisions. Artificial Intelligence (AI) is taking that next level of discovery and allowing machines to learn and help you predict decisions. Centrex Solutions uses its industry and technical expertise to help define the business functions necessary to provide executives with useful Business and Artificial Intelligence tools.

Internet of Things

The Internet of Things (IoT) provides companies the ability to link sensors to almost everything at low costs. This new ability will allow companies to take advantage of the 24/7 capabilities of monitoring items that were previously done manually.
